AHiking & Mountain Climbing

Going on top of the mountain replenished all my anxiety and worries of the future.

I live in a country named Indonesia. We are famous due to its mountains and beaches. The former is probably not the most famous, but it is very important in my life journey. I first started hiking and climbing mountains when I was 8 years old.

Back then, 3 hours of hiking was the hardest thing I could ever imagined to do. Now, 8 hours of non-stop climbing to one of the highest mountains in asia feels like nothing. Many asked how could I feel good when I almost die at the same time ? The answer is very simple, and it is peace. When I am up there, I could feel all my worries, pain, and even mental depression washing away trough the clouds.

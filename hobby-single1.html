Plastic Modelling & Painting

I love modelling, particularly the ones with history in it

Since I was a kid, I was always been fascinated by history. Book by book, picture by pictures, I never did find anything that could "excrete" the feeling of wanting to be in that historic moment.

One day, I was tunneling trough a toy store when I encounter one of these plastic models. I fell in love instantly. Since then, I have been building and painting of tanks, ships, and even small mini-figures.
